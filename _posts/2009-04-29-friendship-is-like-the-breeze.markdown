> Friendship is like the breeze, 
> 
> You can't hold it,
> 
> Smell it,
> 
> Taste it, 
> 
> Or know when it's coming, 
> 
> But you can always feel it, 
> 
> And you'll always know it's there, 
> 
> It may come and then go,
> 
> But you can know it'll always be back.
>
